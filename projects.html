Projects

PID Tuning of Drone

Spring 2025

Tuned and calibrated PID parameters in QGroundControl to improve flight stability and control responsiveness. Conducted structured test flights, analyzed logs, and isolated issues including rate-gain imbalance, interference, and sensor noise.

Refined rate and attitude control loops through controlled parameter sweeps, reducing oscillations and achieving smoother, more predictable flight behaviour. Designed and implemented a test rig to improve safety and reduce risk of airframe damage during testing.

Stewart (Hexapod) Platform

Summer 2025

Designed, modelled, and fabricated a six-degree-of-freedom Stewart platform using FreeCAD. Iterated linkage geometry and structural components to improve motion range and rigidity.

Developed Arduino-based control code and a laptop user interface to coordinate six servo actuators and enable precise platform motion.
