About Me

I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Applied Science in Mechatronics and Robotics Engineering at Queen’s University (Class of 2028). My interests lie in aerospace systems, avionics, and systems integration, with a strong focus on applying theory through hands-on design, testing, and iteration.

Through my involvement with Queen’s Aerospace Design Team, I have developed experience working across electrical, software, mechanical, and systems teams to deliver reliable, flight-ready solutions. I am particularly drawn to roles that combine technical depth with team leadership and coordination.
